Privacy Policy for Lights Out - Classic Puzzle Game

Last updated: January 27, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Lights Out is designed to protect your privacy. We collect minimal information:

  • Game Progress Data: Your game statistics, achievements, and settings are stored locally on your device only
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifying information
  • No Location Data: We do not access your device's location
  • No Camera/Microphone: We do not access your camera or microphone

2. Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms:

  • Game statistics and achievements
  • User preferences and settings
  • Game progress and high scores

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

3. Third-Party Services

Currently, Lights Out does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Data Security

Your game data is encrypted and stored securely on your device. Since no data is transmitted over the internet, there are no network security concerns.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for children as we do not collect any personal information from users of any age.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will notify users through app updates and post the new policy here.
